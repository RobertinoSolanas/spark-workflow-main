# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of:

- age
- body size
- disability
- ethnicity
- gender identity and expression
- level of experience
- nationality
- personal appearance
- race
- religion
- sexual identity and orientation

We are committed to creating an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

---

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Being respectful and considerate in communication
- Being open to different viewpoints and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment or discrimination of any kind
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others’ private information without permission
- Any conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

---

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior.

Maintainers have the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct.

Maintainers may take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any behavior they deem inappropriate.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces, including:

- Git repositories
- Issue trackers
- Pull requests
- Community discussions
- Communication channels related to the project

It also applies when an individual is officially representing the project in public spaces.

---

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it to the project maintainers.

When reporting an issue, please include:

- A description of the incident
- Links or references to relevant communication
- Any additional context that may help resolve the situation

All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

Maintainers are obligated to respect the privacy and security of the reporter.

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may take the following actions depending on the severity of the violation:

- Warning or request for behavior change
- Temporary suspension from the project
- Permanent ban from the project community

Enforcement decisions are made at the discretion of the maintainers.
